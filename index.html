About USTHB

The University of Science and Technology Houari Boumediene (USTHB), founded in 1974 with UNESCO's support, is Algeria's top university for science and technology. Located in Bab Ezzouar, Algiers, it covers over 100 hectares and is known for its excellence in research and education. Many leading scientists have studied or taught here, including Dr. Belkacem Haba, Mokhtar Sellami, and Noureddine Melikechi. USTHB remains a national leader in training engineers, researchers, and innovators across various scientific fields.
